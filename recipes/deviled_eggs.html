Dick's Deviled Eggs

Image Source: Bless This Mess

Classic low-fuss deviled eggs

These deviled eggs are a cherished treat at Christmas for my family. My grandpa made these every year, and I always had to sneak one or two before dinner. They are so good!

Ingredients

  • yolks from boiled eggs
  • whites from boiled eggs - halved to hold deviled yolk mixture
  • Kraft mayonnaise - it's gotta be Kraft
  • apple cider vinegar - 1 tsp at a time
  • yellow mustard - 1 tsp at a time
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil eggs and allow to cool in refrigerator for several hours.
  2. Add egg yolks, mayo, apple cider vinegar, and mustard to the bowl.
  3. Mash and mix the ingredients together.
  4. Add mayo, apple cider vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Scoop heaping spoons of yolk mix into egg white halves.
